üîë Passwords

üõ°Ô∏è Firewalls

Importance: Blocks malicious traffic and prevents hackers from accessing your network. Acts as a barrier between trusted and untrusted networks.

ü¶† Antivirus Software

Importance: Detects and removes malware before it damages systems or steals data. Provides real-time protection.

Features: Virus scanning, malware removal, automatic updates, real-time monitoring, quarantine infected files.

üåê Content Filtering

Importance: Blocks access to harmful websites, protects children from inappropriate content, prevents phishing attacks and malware downloads.
