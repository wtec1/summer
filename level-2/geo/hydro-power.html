3Ô∏è‚É£ Generation and Distribution of Power (Nkula / Tedzani)

  • Water is stored or diverted towards turbines.
  • Fast-moving water turns turbines.
  • Turbines rotate generators to produce electricity.
  • Electricity is stepped up using transformers.
  • Power is transmitted through transmission lines to substations.
  • Electricity is distributed to homes and industries.

4Ô∏è‚É£ Challenges of Hydro-Electric Power in Malawi

  • Low water levels during dry seasons
  • Siltation due to deforestation
  • Old and damaged infrastructure
  • High transmission losses
  • Vandalism of power lines

Possible Solutions:

  • Afforestation and catchment protection
  • Upgrading power stations and lines
  • Using alternative energy sources
  • Public awareness on power protection
