A soil profile is a vertical section of soil showing different layers from the surface downwards.

  • Topsoil (A horizon): Rich in humus and nutrients.
  • Subsoil (B horizon): Contains minerals washed down.
  • Parent rock (C horizon): Weathered rock material.
