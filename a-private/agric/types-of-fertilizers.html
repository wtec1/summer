4Ô∏è‚É£ Advantages and Disadvantages of Organic and Inorganic Fertilizers

Advantages of organic fertilizers

  • improve soil structure
  • increase water-holding capacity
  • environmentally friendly

Disadvantages of organic fertilizers

  • slow nutrient release
  • bulky and hard to transport

Advantages of inorganic fertilizers

  • quick supply of nutrients
  • increase yield rapidly

Disadvantages of inorganic fertilizers

  • can cause soil acidity
  • expensive
  • may pollute water if misused
