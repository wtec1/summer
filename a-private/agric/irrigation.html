‚úì Advantages:

5Ô∏è‚É£ Drip Irrigation System

Water drips slowly and directly to plant roots through tubes and emitters.

‚úì Advantages:

  • Most water-efficient system

  • Precise water application
  • Can use with fertilizers

‚úó Limitations:

  • Requires clean water
  • Needs regular maintenance
  • Technical expertise required

üìù Summary

Irrigation is vital for crop production. The three main systems are: surface (flooding, furrow, basin), overhead (sprinkler), and drip irrigation. Each has advantages and limitations. Choose a system based on topography, water source, crop type, capital, and technical knowledge. Consider soil type, weather, and plant growth stage when determining irrigation frequency.
