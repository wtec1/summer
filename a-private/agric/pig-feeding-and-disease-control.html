Pigs can suffer from many diseases and parasites such as worms, lice and mange. Farmers must prevent and control them to keep pigs healthy and productive.

‚úîÔ∏è Disease and parasite control measures

  • regular deworming
  • proper vaccination where applicable
  • disinfecting pig houses and equipment
  • keeping houses clean and dry
  • isolating sick animals
  • proper disposal of dung and waste
  • good hygiene for workers

‚úîÔ∏è Practical health practices

  • iron injection for young piglets
  • deworming piglets and adults
  • bathing pigs to remove external parasites
  • spraying or dipping against lice and mange
  • regular cleaning and disinfection of housing

üìù Summary

Feeding pigs correctly at each stage promotes fast growth and high production. Types of feeds include creep feed, weaner mash and pig finisher. Protecting pigs from diseases and parasites involves deworming, vaccination, disinfection, proper hygiene and housing. Good feeding and disease control increase pig health, production and farmer income.
