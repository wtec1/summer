A good pig house provides comfort, health, hygiene, and security to pigs.

  • well ventilated to allow fresh air
  • enough space for movement
  • strong walls and floor to prevent escape
  • easy to clean and drain
  • dry floor to avoid diseases
  • proper roofing to protect from rain and sun
  • safe from predators and thieves
  • separate pens for boars, sows, and piglets
