A polygon is a closed two-dimensional shape made up of straight line segments. Polygons are named according to the number of sides they have. Understanding polygon properties helps us solve many geometric problems.

Common Polygons:

  • Triangle: 3 sides
  • Quadrilateral: 4 sides (square, rectangle, trapezium, parallelogram)
  • Pentagon: 5 sides
  • Hexagon: 6 sides
  • Heptagon: 7 sides
  • Octagon: 8 sides
  • Nonagon: 9 sides
  • Decagon: 10 sides
