The Iron Age is the historical period when people used tools and implements made of iron.

  • Archaeology: study of remains from the past.
  • Artefacts: objects left by past people such as potsherds, iron fragments, tools and bones.
  • Archaeologists: people who excavate and study artefacts.
  • Excavation: digging to uncover artefacts.
  • Radiocarbon dating: method of finding the age of artefacts using carbon-14.
