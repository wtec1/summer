ğŸ§ª Photosynthesis Experiments (JCE Standard)

1. Test for Starch in a Leaf

Materials: Green leaf, beaker, water, alcohol, iodine solution, forceps

Procedure:

  • Boil the leaf in water to kill cells.
  • Boil the leaf in alcohol to remove chlorophyll.
  • Rinse the leaf in warm water.
  • Add iodine solution to the leaf.

Result: Leaf turns blue-black, showing starch is present.

2. Experiment to Show Light Is Needed for Photosynthesis

Materials: Potted plant, black paper, clips, iodine solution

Procedure:

  • Cover part of a leaf with black paper.
  • Place plant in sunlight for several hours.
  • Test leaf for starch using iodine.

Result: Only exposed parts turn blue-black, showing light is required.

3. Experiment to Show Chlorophyll Is Required

Materials: Variegated leaf, iodine solution, alcohol

Procedure:

  • Expose variegated leaf to sunlight.
  • Remove chlorophyll using alcohol.
  • Add iodine solution.

Result: Only green areas turn blue-black, showing chlorophyll is needed.

4. Experiment to Show Oxygen Is Produced

Materials: Aquatic plant, water, funnel, test tube, glowing splint

Procedure:

  • Place aquatic plant in water under a funnel.
  • Collect gas in an inverted test tube.
  • Insert glowing splint into the gas.

Result: Splint relights, confirming oxygen is produced.
