3Ô∏è‚É£ Base Interest Rate and Effective Interest Rate

Base interest rate

The minimum interest charged by a financial institution before extra fees are added.

Effective interest rate

The real rate a borrower pays after including all charges such as service fees, insurance, and processing costs.

4Ô∏è‚É£ Calculating Cost of Borrowing

When farmers borrow, they must know:

  • interest to be paid
  • total cost of borrowing

  • whether a profit or loss will be made

Calculating these helps a farmer decide if taking a loan is beneficial.
