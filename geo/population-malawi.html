Population distribution refers to how people are spread across the country.

People concentrate in areas with:

  • fertile soils
  • good climate
  • water availability
  • employment opportunities
  • good transport and communication
  • social services such as schools and hospitals

Areas with low population include:

  • mountainous regions
  • national parks and game reserves
  • very dry areas

3Ô∏è‚É£ Population Density in Malawi

Population density is the number of people living per square kilometer.

Population density is influenced by:

  • fertility of soils
  • availability of jobs
  • relief and terrain
  • climate
  • transport network

High population density occurs in urban areas like Blantyre, Lilongwe, Zomba and Mzuzu. Low density occurs in remote and mountainous areas.
